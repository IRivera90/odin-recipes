Cheesiest Grilled Cheese

Description

The cheesiest of grilled cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread.
  • 2 teaspoons of mayonaise.
  • 2 teaspoons of butter.
  • As many types of cheeses (sliced or shredded) as desired.
  • (optional) Hot sauce.

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan up to medium-high heat.
  2. Spread mayo on outside faces of bread slices.
  3. Add desired cheeses to the inside faces of bread slices.
  4. Melt butter on frying pan.
  5. Place sandwich on frying pan.
  6. Flip once cheese begins melting and until both sides of sandwich are golden brown.
  7. Serve with tomato soup if desired.

Tuscan Chicken

Description

A creamy Goobers specialty. Sliced chicken breasts with alfredo sauce, sun dried tomatoes, garlic, and onions, slow cooked to perfection and topped with spinach.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts/tenders
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed red chili pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 can of sun dried tomatoes
  • 1 jar of alfredo sauce
  • 2 cups of spinach

Steps

  1. Add chicken breasts to slow cooker on high
  2. Mince garlic cloves and set aside
  3. Dice onion and set aside
  4. In a bowl, mix jar of alfredo sauce, red chili pepper flakes, italian seasoning, minced garlic, diced onion, sun dried tomatoes, grated parmesan cheese, pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Pour alfredo sauce mixture evenly over the chicken breasts and cook for 3-4 hours on high.
  6. 10 minutes before the timer is up, add in the chopped spinach and mix until wilted.
  7. Serve over rice or pasta.
